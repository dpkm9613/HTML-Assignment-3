Testimonials

" We did Electric Run for the first time this past summer. We have heard amazing things about it, and after seeing all the pictures and facebook posts from the event in other cities, we were super excited. In case you know nothing about Electric Run, it's basically a 5k at night in a course that has been designed to simulate running through multiple "worlds" - areas that each have their own style and environment, filled with colorful lights, decorations, music and photo areas. "
