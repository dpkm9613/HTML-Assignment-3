Frequently Asked Questions

Is Electric Run a rave?
- No. The Electric Run will feature many of the same light elements that could be found at a rave but the focus of Electric Run is on health, fitness, and celebrating with friends. We believe everyone, no matter their age, should be able to experience music and lights. in an environment that is healthy and happy. There is no tolerance for drugs at the event.

Is this a timed event?
- Nope. This is not a “race”, this is an experience. Electric Run is all about feeling healthy, having fun, dancing your heart out, and spending an amazing night out with your friends. As you make your way through each Dance Party Zones, we encourage you to hang out, dance and make it a fun, memorable night.

How do you feel about walkers?
- Walkers are very welcome! We can’t blame you for wanting to take it slow and dance the night away….we just ask that if you choose to walk, stay to the right so runners can pass safely to your left.

Should I bring my own glow stuff?
- Shop for LED and glow gear at the Electric Wear store. Come let your light shine and your creative juices flow while adding your own creative spark by dressing up in costumes that glow, shine, blink and dazzle. Show us what you’ve got!

Are there refunds?
- Unfortunately, we cannot issue refunds. The logistics of setting up and planning the event make it impossible. Just make sure you are good to go before signing up. If for some reason you can’t make it to race day, at least come to the check in, you can still come and pick up your Electric Run gear, or see below how to transfer your registration.

Can someone else pick up my packet for me?
- Yep! You just have to send them with a copy of your confirmation email (printed or electronic) with your unique barcode to pick up your goods!
